New York to Mumbai 2016

The Challenge

Road Trip

For this journey, we are going to use our 2009 Nissan Versa Hatchback. Nissian Versa's are not known for their adventure readiness. We will demonstrate that this hatchback can take on all the challenges that the silk road can throw at a car.

Route

Flying from New York to London and then driving to Mumbai, we will be passing through 15 countries along the Silk Route - England, France, Germany, Poland, Belarus, Latvia, Lithuania, Estonia, Russia, Kazakhstan, China, Nepal, India.

Charity

Kedi Residential High School provides education, boarding, meal, clothing, and medical care to more than 100 girls of economically deprived tribal areas. In this project, the team will aim to raise $5000 for scholarships to help children advance their studies.
