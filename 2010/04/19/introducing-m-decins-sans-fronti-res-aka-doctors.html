Introducing Médecins Sans Frontières aka Doctors Without Borders

April 19, 2010

by Stephen Jan in New York

We are thrilled to announce that fund raising is going really well. We have reached the event minimum requirement of 1000 UK pounds last night and will now be raising money for MSF, aka Doctors Without Borders. Given that we will be taking an ambulance across Europe and Asia and the recent events in Haiti, Chile, and China, we felt that adding a cause such as MSF would be a fitting.
